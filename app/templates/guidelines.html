Important Notes

  • The Power Denoise web application could only be used on devices with GPU. If your device does not have GPU, please seek assistance from the project team.
  • Only one image is accepted at once.
  • Remember to download your image once denoised. Once the page refreshes, the input and output would be erased.

Image Guidelines

  • The image input is expected to be a sandstorm image.
  • Acceptable image formats:
    • Windows bitmaps: *.bmp, *.dib
    • JPEG files: *.jpeg, *.jpg, *.jpe
    • JPEG 2000 files: *.jp2
    • Portable Network Graphics: *.png
    • WebP: *.webp
    • AVIF: *.avif
    • Portable image format: *.pbm, *.pgm, *.ppm, *.pxm, *.pnm
    • PFM files: *.pfm
    • Sun rasters: *.sr, *.ras
    • TIFF files: *.tiff, *.tif
    • OpenEXR Image files: *.exr
    • Radiance HDR: *.hdr, *.pic
    • Raster and Vector geospatial data supported by GDAL
  • Individual image files should not exceed 10 MB.
  • Maintain the original aspect ratio of the image.
  • Remove any watermarks or text overlays from the image.
  • Ensure image size has larger than xx x xx

Steps

  • Go to DehazeGAN navigation tab
  • Click on upload image. Make sure the image uploaded is as per the "Image Guidelines" set above.
  • A preview of the image uploaded would be shown. Once confirmed, click on Denoise.
  • You can download your image by clicking on the Download button. If you do not download your image, your image would be erased once the page reloads.
